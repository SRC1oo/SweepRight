Our Story

Built for busy people across the UK

SweepRight was founded by a team right here in the UK who were frustrated with the unreliable, overpriced, and opaque home cleaning market. We knew there had to be a better way.

We built a tech platform that takes the hassle out of everything — from finding a trustworthy cleaner to managing recurring bookings, payments, and scheduling. All in one place, from your phone.

Today we serve thousands of happy households across the UK, from studio flats in city centres to family homes in the suburbs — and we're just getting started.

Our Vetting Process

Every cleaner earns your trust

Only 1 in 5 applicants makes it onto the SweepRight platform. Here's how we screen them:

1

Application Review

Full work history, references, and ID verification reviewed by our team.

2

Criminal Record Check

Background screening through authorised UK government databases.

3

In-Person Interview

Face-to-face interview assessing professionalism, communication, and reliability.

4

Practical Assessment

Hands-on cleaning test to verify quality standards and technique before going live.
